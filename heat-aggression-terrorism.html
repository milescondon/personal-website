Collectivism and the Heat–Aggression Relationship in Terrorism

Does culture shape the well-documented link between heat and aggression? Analyzing decades of global data, this study shows that societies with stronger in-group and family ties exhibit a weaker relationship between rising temperatures and terrorist attacks.
